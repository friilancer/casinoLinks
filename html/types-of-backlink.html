5 Types of backlink and their impact on seo

What you need to know

Different types of backlink to improve your ranking

Backlinks exist in a wide variety of types and quality, getting familiar with the different types of backlink will be helpful for the success of a website for search engines, the type of backlink you acquire to your site can either impact your site positively or negatively. Let us look at the different types of backlink and their impact on SEO

Editorial links

Editorial links are hyperlinks from very popular and high-quality websites like Forbes , New York times, CNN etc to your website. These types of links are the most valuable for SEO as they occur naturally within the content of the webpage.

For one to earn this type of link it must really be true that you have high quality and relevant content which is worth mentioning on those popular sites and being mentioned on those sites will relatively increase your traffic. One editorial link from relevant sites is better than 10 links from irrelevant sites.

Manual outreach link

These are backlinks earned from websites that do not have a high DA as the editorial links, they are earned through nurturing relationships with the webmaster or who has a website which is in the same niche as yours where you can offer to provide some services to them which will make them link to you. This type of link is very good for SEO and drives medium traffic to your website.

Web 2.0 links

These are sites which allows user-generated content to be created on their subdomain for free some examples of web2.0 links are wordpress.com,blogger.com,youtube etc.these types of links are very effective for SEO.

PBN link

Private Blog Network, these are links on websites that you create and manage the content. PBN links are essential for SEO if used in the right way but could attract a penalty if Google realizes that you are linking to PBN sites. Getting links from a private network is easy and is very powerful when trying to rank affiliate sites. A private blog network with a high DA optimized content will be very helpful in your link building campaign as it will save time from going round to ask new websites to link to you.

Linkless backlink

Linkless backlinks are mentions of your business, product or service without any hyperlink to your webpage, Google considers linkless backlink as a positive ranking signal because it cannot easily be manipulated if a company or a person does not trust your brand it cannot mention you in his post. This linkess mention improves your brand popularity, it makes more impact on SEO if the domain that mentioned your brand is authoritative and trustworthy.the more people are mentioning your brand the more likely it will get a valuable boost.
